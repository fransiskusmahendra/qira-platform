# PRD-0055 — AI Analytics & Insights

**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Status:** Draft  
**Priority:** Critical  
**Owner:** AI Intelligence Team

---

# Overview

AI Analytics & Insights is the enterprise intelligence platform of QIRA.

It transforms operational data, AI activities, workflows, customer interactions, and business events into actionable insights through dashboards, reports, predictive analytics, and AI-generated recommendations.

Rather than simply visualizing historical data, the platform continuously analyzes organizational behavior and recommends improvements.

AI Analytics & Insights serves as the decision intelligence layer across the entire QIRA ecosystem.

---

# Vision

Empower organizations to make faster and smarter decisions using AI-driven insights.

Every business decision should be supported by real-time data, predictive intelligence, and explainable AI recommendations.

---

# Objectives

The platform should:

- Centralize business analytics
- Monitor operational performance
- Analyze AI utilization
- Predict business trends
- Detect anomalies
- Generate executive insights
- Support custom dashboards
- Deliver scheduled reports
- Enable data-driven decision making
