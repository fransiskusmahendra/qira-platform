# PRD-0039 — Industry Solution Packs

**Document ID:** PRD-0039

**Version:** 1.0.0

**Status:** Vision

**Priority:** High

**Owner:** Product Solutions Team

---

# Purpose

Industry Solution Packs provide pre-configured AI Employees, workflows, automations, dashboards, templates, prompts, integrations, and knowledge bases tailored for specific industries.

Organizations can deploy a complete solution in minutes instead of building everything from scratch.

---

# Vision

Enable organizations to accelerate AI adoption through ready-to-use industry solutions built on the QIRA platform.

---

# Objectives

The platform should:

- Reduce implementation time
- Deliver best practices
- Standardize deployments
- Simplify onboarding
- Support industry-specific workflows
- Accelerate customer success
- Encourage marketplace adoption

---

# Solution Pack Structure

Each Solution Pack contains:

- AI Employees
- Workflow Templates
- Automation Rules
- Dashboard Templates
- Prompt Templates
- Knowledge Base
- Reports
- Integrations
- Documentation
- Sample Data (Optional)
