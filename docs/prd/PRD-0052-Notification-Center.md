# PRD-0052 — Notification Center

**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Status:** Draft  
**Priority:** High  
**Owner:** Platform Communication Team

---

# Overview

The Notification Center is the centralized communication platform for the QIRA ecosystem.

It delivers notifications generated by business events, AI Employees, workflows, integrations, and user activities through multiple communication channels.

Instead of every module implementing its own notification mechanism, all notifications are managed through a unified service.

The Notification Center ensures messages are delivered to the right recipient, through the right channel, at the right time.

---

# Vision

Provide intelligent, reliable, and personalized communication across every interaction within QIRA.

Users should receive only meaningful notifications through their preferred channels without being overwhelmed.

---

# Objectives

The Notification Center should:

- Deliver notifications across multiple channels
- Support real-time and scheduled delivery
- Personalize communication preferences
- Prevent notification fatigue
- Track delivery status
- Enable AI-generated notifications
- Support enterprise communication policies
- Scale to millions of notifications per day

---

# Problems

Enterprise applications generate thousands of events every day.

Examples include:

- Workflow approvals
- New tasks
- AI recommendations
- Customer inquiries
- Security alerts
- Invoice approvals
- Meeting reminders
- Contract expirations
- System incidents

Without centralized management:

- Users receive duplicate notifications
- Important messages are missed
- Delivery becomes inconsistent
- Communication cannot be audited

---
