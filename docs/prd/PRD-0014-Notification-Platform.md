# PRD-0014 — Notification Platform

**Document ID:** PRD-0014  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Status:** Draft  
**Priority:** Critical  
**Owner:** Platform Engineering Team

---

# Purpose

The Notification Platform is the centralized communication service for the QIRA ecosystem.

Every notification sent by QIRA—whether generated by users, AI Employees, workflows, projects, billing, or system events—must pass through this platform.

The Notification Platform provides reliable, configurable, multi-channel delivery while respecting user preferences, organization policies, and notification priorities.

---

# Vision

Build an enterprise-grade notification service capable of delivering the right message to the right user through the right channel at the right time.

The platform should be channel-agnostic, event-driven, and highly reliable.

---

# Objectives

The Notification Platform should:

- Centralize notification delivery
- Support multiple communication channels
- Respect user notification preferences
- Deliver notifications reliably
- Retry failed deliveries
- Track delivery status
- Prevent duplicate notifications
- Support scheduled delivery
- Support AI-generated notifications
- Provide complete audit history
