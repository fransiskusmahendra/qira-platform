# PRD-0040 — Partner Ecosystem Platform

**Document ID:** PRD-0040

**Version:** 1.0.0

**Status:** Vision

**Priority:** High

**Owner:** Business Development Team

---

# Purpose

The Partner Ecosystem Platform enables external organizations and professionals to sell, implement, customize, support, and extend the QIRA platform.

Partners become an extension of the QIRA team, helping customers successfully adopt AI solutions while expanding the reach of the platform.

---

# Vision

Create a global ecosystem where trusted partners help organizations successfully implement and grow with QIRA.

---

# Objectives

The platform should:

- Manage partner organizations
- Support partner onboarding
- Enable implementation services
- Support referrals
- Manage certifications
- Track partner performance
- Enable revenue sharing
- Support marketplace publishing
- Grow the QIRA ecosystem

---

# Partner Types

Support multiple partner categories:

- Implementation Partner
- Technology Partner
- Solution Partner
- Referral Partner
- Training Partner
- Managed Service Provider (MSP)
- Independent Consultant
- Educational Partner

Organizations may hold multiple partner roles.

# Core Features

## Partner Portal

Partners can:

- Manage customers
- View projects
- Submit opportunities
- Access documentation
- Download marketing materials
- Publish marketplace assets
- Track certifications

---

## Lead Registration

Partners may:

- Register opportunities
- Track opportunity status
- Collaborate with QIRA
- Protect registered deals

---

## Customer Management

Partners can:

- View assigned customers
- Manage implementations
- Monitor deployments
- Track adoption
- Submit support requests

---

## Marketplace Publishing

Partners may publish:

- AI Employees
- Solution Packs
- Workflow Templates
- Integrations
- Prompt Libraries
- Dashboards

Published assets follow the Marketplace approval process.

---

- Marketplace publication under 24 hours
