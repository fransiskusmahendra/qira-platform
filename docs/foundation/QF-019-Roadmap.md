# Purpose

This document defines the strategic roadmap of the QIRA Platform.

The roadmap aligns business strategy, engineering priorities, AI capabilities, and platform evolution.

This roadmap is directional rather than date-driven.

Priorities may change as customer needs evolve.

The long-term vision does not.

---

# Roadmap Philosophy

QIRA is built incrementally.

Every release should:

- Improve customer value.
- Improve platform capability.
- Improve engineering quality.
- Improve AI intelligence.
- Improve reusable assets.

Shipping continuously is preferred over infrequent large releases.

---

# Long-Term Vision

The long-term objective is to become the operating platform for AI-enabled organizations.

Organizations should be able to:

- Discover opportunities.
- Build AI Employees.
- Deploy AI Employees.
- Manage AI Employees.
- Improve AI Employees.

All from one platform.

# Strategic Phases

The roadmap consists of seven phases.

---

# Phase 1 — Foundation

Objective

Build the platform foundation.

Deliverables

- Company Foundation
- Documentation
- Engineering Standards
- Design System
- Authentication
- Organization Management
- Workspace Framework
- Company Brain Foundation

Success Criteria

The platform architecture is stable.

---

# Phase 2 — Public Platform

Objective

Launch the public-facing platform.

Modules

- Marketing Website
- AI Receptionist
- AI Consultant
- Service Catalog
- Industry Pages
- Contact
- Discovery Request

Success Criteria

Visitors can understand QIRA and initiate Discovery.

---

# Phase 3 — Client Workspace

Objective

Create a collaborative workspace for customers.

Modules

- Dashboard
- Discovery
- AI Interview
- Digital Data Room
- Deliverables
- Proposal
- Timeline
- AI Chat

Success Criteria

A client can complete the Discovery process entirely online.

---

# Phase 4 — Internal Workspace

Objective

Enable QIRA consultants to deliver projects efficiently.

Modules

- CRM
- Lead Management
- Discovery Manager
- Proposal Studio
- AI Business Analyst
- AI Solution Architect
- Project Manager
- Billing

Success Criteria

End-to-end project delivery is managed within QIRA.

---

# Phase 5 — Company Brain

Objective

Transform QIRA into a continuously learning platform.

Modules

- Knowledge Graph
- Prompt Library
- Pattern Library
- Semantic Search
- AI Memory
- Context Builder
- Decision Intelligence

Success Criteria

Every completed project improves future projects.

---

# Phase 6 — AI Marketplace

Objective

Allow organizations to install AI Employees.

Marketplace Examples

- AI Receptionist
- AI Sales
- AI HR
- AI Finance
- AI Legal
- AI Procurement
- AI Marketing
- AI Customer Success

Success Criteria

Organizations can deploy AI Employees without custom development.

---

# Phase 7 — Global Platform

Objective

Expand internationally.

Capabilities

- Multi-language
- Multi-currency
- Multi-region
- Regional compliance
- White-label platform
- Partner ecosystem

Success Criteria

QIRA supports customers globally.

# Guiding Principles

Every roadmap decision should:

Improve the platform.

Increase reusability.

Reduce implementation effort.

Strengthen the Company Brain.

Create customer value.

Support long-term scalability.

---

# Things We Will NOT Build

To maintain focus, QIRA will avoid:

- One-off client applications.
- Features with no reusable value.
- Technology experiments without business value.
- Duplicate modules.
- Vendor-specific architecture.
- AI features without measurable outcomes.

---

# Roadmap Governance

The roadmap is reviewed:

- Quarterly for execution priorities.
- Annually for strategic direction.

Major changes require approval from Product and Technology leadership.
