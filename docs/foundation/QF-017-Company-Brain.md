# Purpose

This document defines the architecture, governance, lifecycle, and operational standards of the QIRA Company Brain.

The Company Brain is the most valuable strategic asset of QIRA.

Unlike traditional CRMs or Knowledge Bases, the Company Brain continuously learns from every customer, every project, every implementation, every AI interaction, and every engineering decision.

The Company Brain ensures that QIRA becomes smarter after every engagement.

---

# Vision

The Company Brain is the collective intelligence of QIRA.

It remembers what humans forget.

It connects knowledge that humans cannot easily connect.

It continuously improves every AI Employee and every future project.

---

# Core Philosophy

Every activity creates knowledge.

Every knowledge improves the platform.

Every platform improvement benefits every future customer.

Knowledge is never considered complete.

Knowledge continuously evolves.

# Customer Isolation

Customer knowledge is isolated.

One customer cannot access another customer's information.

Shared patterns are anonymized before becoming Company Brain assets.

---

# Versioning

Knowledge is never overwritten.

Every change creates a new version.

History must remain available.
