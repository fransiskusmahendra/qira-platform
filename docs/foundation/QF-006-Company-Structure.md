# Purpose

This document defines how QIRA is organized as an AI-First company.

Unlike traditional companies, QIRA is designed so that Human Employees and AI Employees work together as one organization.

Every responsibility, workflow, and decision should clearly identify whether it is owned by:

- Human
- AI
- Human + AI

---

# Organization Philosophy

QIRA is built around three layers.

```

Leadership

↓

Business

↓

Engineering

```

Each layer is supported by AI Employees.
