# Purpose

This document defines the official business model of QIRA.

It explains how QIRA creates value, delivers value, captures value, and continuously improves its platform.

Every strategic, commercial, product, and engineering decision should align with this business model.

---

# Business Philosophy

QIRA is not a software development company.

QIRA is an AI Transformation Platform.

We do not sell software.

We help organizations become AI-enabled.

Software is one of many tools.

Business outcomes are the product.

---

# Value Proposition

QIRA enables organizations to deploy AI Employees that work alongside Human Employees.

Customers receive:

- AI Employees
- Business Automation
- Digital Transformation
- AI Consulting
- Knowledge Platforms
- Business Intelligence
- Workflow Automation
- Custom Business Applications

Every engagement should generate measurable business value.
