# THE QIRA FOUNDATION

Version: 1.0 Status: Foundational Edition

> This document is the Single Source of Truth (SSOT) for QIRA.

# 1. Identity

QIRA is an AI-First Digital Transformation Company. QIRA builds AI
Employees, reusable AI platforms, and digital transformation solutions.
QIRA is not a software house, chatbot vendor, or automation agency.

# 2. Vision

Enable every organization to deploy AI Employees alongside human
employees.

# 3. Mission

-   Build reusable AI platforms.
-   Reduce repetitive work.
-   Improve customer experience.
-   Preserve organizational knowledge.
-   Make AI practical.
